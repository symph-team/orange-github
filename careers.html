Recruitment Process Q&A

Interested to send in your application? Please read the FAQ to get acquainted with our screening process.

How do I apply for a job vacancy?

Kindly fill-out the online application form and then submit your resume to careers@orangeandbronze.com and indicate the position in the subject line.

What's the next step after submitting my resume?

You will receive an e-mail acknowledgement about your job application. If you are qualified for the position, you will be contacted by a recruiter to invite you to take our examination.

What happens during the recruitment process?

The interview process is very rigorous. It includes a technical, behavioral and sometimes, client interview. You’ll be asked a series of questions about your academic and work background. You should also be able to elaborate on your answers by providing extant details regarding your previous accomplishments. You’ll be allowed to ask questions at the end of the interview.

How will I know if I passed or failed the exam/interview?

You’ll be notified through e-mail whether you passed or failed the exam/interview. It usually takes us a week to process your application. We suggest that you look at your e-mail inbox from time to time.

What is the next step after the interview?

Upon passing the series of interviews, the next step would be the job offer meeting. A recruiter will coordinate with you to ask your availability.

If I have more questions regarding my application, where could I send my concerns?

Just email careers@orangeandbronze.com for your application concerns.
